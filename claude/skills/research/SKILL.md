# Research

## When To Use

Use when policy, documentation, current facts, or source comparison matters.

## Core Responsibility

Collect traceable facts and separate sourced evidence from interpretation.

## Input Expectations

- research question
- source scope
- output form

## Output Expectations

- traceable facts
- concise synthesis
- clear distinction between source and inference

## Boundary Rules

- do not present inference as quoted fact
- hand off to writing or review once evidence is stable

## Shared Source

`shared/skills/research/SKILL.md`

## Collaboration Pattern

- pair with `agents/researcher.md`
- hand off to `skills/writing/` or `skills/review/` once facts are confirmed
