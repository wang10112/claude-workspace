# Formal Writing Playbook

## Purpose

This playbook turns recurring formal-writing work into a reusable, low-guesswork capability inside a general dual-AI workspace.

## Default Routing

- drafting or major rewrite: `internal-comms`
- internal summary or leadership note: `internal-comms`
- Chinese polish after structure is correct: `internal-comms -> humanizer-zh`
- formatting-only handoff: `official-doc-format`

## Input Checklist

- document type
- audience
- purpose
- dates and owners
- source facts
- exact wording to preserve

## Output Checklist

- clear structure
- explicit actors and actions
- no invented facts
- correct decision between content and formatting work
