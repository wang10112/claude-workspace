# Research Playbook

## Purpose

Turn recurring research work into a reusable, source-grounded workflow inside the shared workspace.

## Default Research Order

1. Define the question and source scope.
2. Gather sources before synthesizing.
3. Separate sourced facts from interpretation.
4. Record unanswered questions explicitly.
5. Hand off to writing, review, or verification once evidence is stable.

## Research Output Shape

- research question
- sourced findings
- interpretation or synthesis
- unresolved questions

## Good Fit

- policy or documentation lookups
- source-backed briefing work
- framework or product comparison
- fact collection before writing or review

## Bad Fit

- persuasive writing before facts are stable
- presenting inference as if it were quoted evidence
