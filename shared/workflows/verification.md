# Verification Workflow

## Purpose

Provide a neutral workflow for checking whether changes, drafts, or capability surfaces are actually ready.

## Typical Inputs

- target artifact or changed area
- expected behavior or acceptance criteria
- relevant commands, checks, or review focus

## Typical Outputs

- pass or fail summary
- findings and residual risks
- next action recommendation

## Capability Lanes

- review
- research when standards or current facts matter
- testing when behavior needs execution-level confirmation

## Suggested Shared Skill Route

- `verification`
- `review`
- `research` when external references are required
